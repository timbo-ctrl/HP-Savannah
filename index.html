On the philosophy of the house

"A second-generation breeder raised inside the work. Small litters, close handling, and a standard that begins before placement. The point is not more cats. The point is better ones."

— HP Savannahs · Breeding philosophy

What is
High %?

The F1 Savannah is the first-generation cross between an African Serval and a domestic cat. Of every F1, only a rare few exceed 70% Serval blood. Those we call High Percentage.

HP Savannahs produces fewer than a dozen of these cats a year. Each is raised in the home, handled early, and placed only after application review.

The Process
No Shortcuts.

We do not sell cats. We place them. Each application is reviewed personally — home environment, prior experience, long-term intent. When the fit is right, the conversation begins.

01
Step One

Application

A short private form. Your context, your home, your intention. No deposits required at this stage. We respond within 72 hours.

02
Step Two

Consultation

A one-on-one call with the breeder. Transparency on bloodline, temperament, care, and the realities of owning a high-percentage cat.

03
Step Three

Placement

A kitten is matched — not chosen. The breeder selects the individual animal suited to your life. Reservation is secured privately.

04
Step Four

Delivery & Lifetime

White-glove transport worldwide. A relationship, not a transaction — lifetime mentorship for every cat we place.

Trained Beside
Dogs

The cats are not raised in isolation. Joseph Cinnante works with HP Savannahs to build early confidence around dogs, handlers, movement, boundaries, and the rhythm of a real home.
